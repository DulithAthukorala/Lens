Summary: A Colombo restaurant improved local search visibility and online reservations by optimizing mobile page speed and actively responding to Google reviews.

Client: Harbor Table Colombo  
Industry: Restaurant / Café

Problem:
Harbor Table had strong foot traffic but limited online visibility. The Google Business profile had more than 140 reviews but only 12% had responses from the owner. The restaurant’s website also loaded slowly on mobile devices, causing visitors to leave before reaching the reservation page.

Solution:
A review response workflow was implemented so staff could respond to new reviews within 48 hours. The website was optimized by reducing image sizes and improving mobile load performance. Local SEO improvements were also implemented, including structured data and optimized headings targeting “brunch cafe Colombo.”

Result:
Google Business interactions increased 43% over two months. Mobile page load time dropped from 4.4 seconds to 2.0 seconds. Online reservation requests increased from 57 to 83 bookings per month.
