Summary: A Colombo tutoring center increased student enrolments by improving local search visibility and launching a Meta Ads campaign targeting parents of O/L and A/L students.

Client: Apex Academy, Nugegoda
Industry: Private Education / Tutoring Center

Problem:
The tutoring center offered O/L and A/L science and mathematics classes but was operating at 58% classroom capacity. Parents searching for tuition classes in Nugegoda and surrounding areas were not finding the center in Google results. The center had no social media presence and no digital advertising history. All enrolments came from flyers, notice boards, and parent referrals.

Solution:
A Google Business Profile was created and optimised with subject-specific service listings, timetable information, and past student result achievements. On-page SEO was implemented for keywords including "O/L maths tuition Nugegoda" and "A/L chemistry classes Colombo." Meta Ads campaigns were launched targeting parents aged 35–55 within 7km, with creative focused on exam results and class size limits to create urgency.

Result:
Classroom capacity utilisation increased from 58% to 94% within three months. Monthly new enrolment enquiries grew from 9 to 47. Google Maps ranking reached position 2 for primary local keywords. Meta Ads delivered a cost per enrolment enquiry of LKR 320. The center opened a second batch for A/L chemistry to meet demand.
