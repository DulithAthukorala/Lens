Summary: A Colombo-based B2B SaaS startup increased free trial sign-ups and paid conversions after restructuring its website messaging and launching targeted LinkedIn campaigns.

Client: Synqlo Technologies, Colombo 02
Industry: B2B SaaS / HR Software

Problem:
The startup had developed a workforce management platform aimed at mid-size Sri Lankan and regional enterprises, but the website was generating fewer than 20 free trial sign-ups per month. The homepage led with technical feature lists rather than business outcomes, and there was no dedicated landing page for paid acquisition traffic. LinkedIn and Google Ads had been attempted briefly but abandoned due to poor results.

Solution:
The homepage and core landing pages were rewritten around buyer pain points — payroll errors, compliance gaps, and manual leave tracking — rather than product features. A dedicated free trial landing page was built with a single CTA and social proof from existing clients. LinkedIn Lead Gen campaigns were launched targeting HR managers and finance directors at companies with 50–500 employees in Sri Lanka and Malaysia.

Result:
Monthly free trial sign-ups increased from 18 to 74 over four months. Trial-to-paid conversion rate improved from 9% to 22%. LinkedIn cost per qualified lead averaged LKR 1,450. Organic traffic to solution pages grew by 138% following on-page SEO updates.
