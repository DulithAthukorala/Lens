Summary: A Colombo coworking space increased hot desk and private office memberships by improving search visibility and launching a targeted Meta Ads campaign aimed at remote workers and freelancers.

Client: Basecamp Cowork, Colombo 07
Industry: Coworking / Flexible Workspace

Problem:
The space had strong facilities and a central location but was operating at 46% desk occupancy. Awareness was low beyond a small network of early members. The website had no blog content, no structured SEO, and ranked on page 4 for searches such as "coworking space Colombo" and "hot desk Colombo 07." There was no clear digital acquisition strategy in place.

Solution:
Landing pages were created for each membership tier — hot desk, dedicated desk, and private office — with transparent pricing and a virtual tour. Blog content was published targeting freelancer and remote work queries relevant to Sri Lanka. Meta Ads campaigns were launched targeting professionals aged 24–40 in Colombo using interest and behaviour segments. A free one-day trial offer was introduced as the primary conversion action.

Result:
Desk occupancy increased from 46% to 83% within four months. Monthly new membership sign-ups grew from 7 to 31. The free trial campaign converted at 38% from trial to paid membership. Organic traffic to the website increased by 195%. Cost per new paying member via Meta Ads averaged LKR 2,100.
