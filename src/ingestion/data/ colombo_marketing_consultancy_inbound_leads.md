Summary: A Colombo marketing consultancy grew inbound client enquiries by rebuilding its website around case study content and launching a lead magnet targeted at local SME owners.

Client: Meridian Growth Consulting, Colombo 05
Industry: Marketing Consultancy

Problem:
The consultancy was winning new clients almost entirely through personal referrals. Its website attracted fewer than 300 monthly visitors and had no structured mechanism to capture or nurture leads. The firm had deep expertise but no published work demonstrating results, which limited its credibility with prospects outside its existing network.

Solution:
Six client case studies were written and published, each structured around specific measurable outcomes. A free downloadable guide — "Digital Marketing Benchmarks for Sri Lankan SMEs" — was introduced as a lead magnet gated behind an email opt-in form. A monthly email newsletter was launched to nurture captured leads, and Google Search Ads were run for consultancy-related keywords in Colombo.

Result:
Monthly website visitors grew from 290 to 1,640 within five months. The lead magnet generated 310 email subscribers in the first 90 days. Inbound client enquiries increased from an average of 2 per month to 11. Two new retained clients were attributed directly to the newsletter nurture sequence.
