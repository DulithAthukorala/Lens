Summary: A Colombo gym improved membership sign-ups after fixing a slow mobile website and moving the signup form above the fold, increasing leads by 34%.

Client: PulseFit Colombo  
Industry: Fitness Center / Gym

Problem:
PulseFit Colombo relied mainly on Instagram promotions but received very few membership sign-ups through its website. The mobile PageSpeed score was 29, and the membership signup form appeared only after two full scrolls. Organic search visibility was also weak because the website had not published any new content in nearly 11 months.

Solution:
Website performance was improved by compressing large images, removing unused JavaScript, and enabling server-side caching. The membership signup form was moved to the homepage hero section with a clear “Start Your Membership” call-to-action. A simple SEO landing page targeting “gym membership Colombo” was also created.

Result:
Mobile PageSpeed improved from 29 → 81. Organic search traffic increased 34% within three months, and monthly membership inquiries increased from 38 to 63 leads.
