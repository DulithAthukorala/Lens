Summary: A Colombo furniture e-commerce brand improved online sales conversions after restructuring product pages, reducing page load times, and launching Google Shopping campaigns.

Client: Teak & Co., Colombo 10
Industry: Furniture / Home Interiors E-commerce

Problem:
The brand sold locally manufactured teak and rattan furniture online but had a site-wide conversion rate of 0.7%. Product pages lacked room-setting photography, dimension guides, and delivery information, causing high exit rates before checkout. The website loaded in 9.1 seconds on mobile. There were no active paid acquisition channels, and organic traffic was largely unqualified.

Solution:
Product pages were rebuilt with lifestyle photography, detailed dimension tables, material specifications, and a visible delivery timeline for Colombo and outstation orders. Mobile load time was reduced from 9.1 seconds to 2.6 seconds through image optimisation and CDN implementation. Google Shopping campaigns were launched targeting furniture and home interiors searches. A live chat widget was added to assist pre-purchase queries.

Result:
Site-wide conversion rate improved from 0.7% to 2.4%. Mobile PageSpeed score improved from 27 to 73. Monthly online revenue increased by 118% over five months. Google Shopping contributed 38% of total transactions. Average order value increased by LKR 8,400 following the introduction of product bundling recommendations.
