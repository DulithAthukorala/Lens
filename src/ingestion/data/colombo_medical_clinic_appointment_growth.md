Summary: A Colombo private medical clinic increased online appointment bookings after improving local search visibility and introducing a structured digital booking workflow.

Client: Wellcare Medical Centre, Colombo 06
Industry: Healthcare / Private Medical Clinic

Problem:
The clinic offered GP consultations, minor procedures, and specialist referrals but had no online booking capability. Patients were required to call during office hours to schedule appointments, leading to missed calls and lost bookings. The clinic had no Google Business Profile and ranked on page 6 for searches such as "private clinic Colombo 06" and "GP consultation Wellawatte."

Solution:
A Google Business Profile was created and verified with full service listings, consultation hours, and specialist availability. An online appointment booking widget was embedded on the website, integrated with the clinic's scheduling system. On-page local SEO was implemented across service pages. A post-visit SMS was introduced requesting a Google review from patients who consented.

Result:
Online appointment bookings reached 61% of total monthly bookings within four months, up from zero. Monthly new patient registrations increased by 49%. Google Business Profile impressions averaged 5,200 per month. The clinic accumulated 88 verified reviews with an average rating of 4.6 stars. Phone call volume for routine bookings dropped by 34%, reducing front-desk workload.
