Summary: A Colombo law firm grew inbound client enquiries by improving local SEO, publishing practice-area content, and making its website accessible and credible to first-time visitors.

Client: Fernando & Jayawardena, Attorneys-at-Law, Colombo 03
Industry: Legal Services / Law Firm

Problem:
The firm specialised in property law, commercial contracts, and immigration matters but attracted almost no enquiries through digital channels. Its website was outdated, contained no content addressing common legal questions, and loaded poorly on mobile with a PageSpeed score of 26. Prospective clients searching for "property lawyer Colombo" or "Sri Lanka visa lawyer" were not finding the firm.

Solution:
The website was redesigned with clear practice area pages, attorney profiles, and a confidential enquiry form. A content series was published addressing frequently searched legal topics including land deed transfers, company incorporation, and spouse visa applications in Sri Lanka. Local SEO was implemented with structured citations and a fully optimised Google Business Profile. PageSpeed was improved through a full technical audit and theme replacement.

Result:
Organic traffic increased by 312% over five months. Inbound enquiries from the website grew from 3 to 26 per month. PageSpeed score improved from 26 to 74. The firm ranked on page 1 for six target practice-area keywords within four months. Two new retained commercial clients were directly attributed to organic search within the first quarter.
