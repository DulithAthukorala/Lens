Summary: A Colombo real estate agency increased property inquiries by improving SEO structure and optimizing listing pages for search visibility.

Client: UrbanNest Realty Colombo  
Industry: Real Estate Agency

Problem:
UrbanNest Realty relied mostly on property listing portals and received very few inquiries through its own website. Property pages lacked detailed descriptions and SEO structure, causing most listings to rank beyond the first page of search results.

Solution:
Each property listing was rewritten with structured content, neighborhood insights, and optimized titles targeting keywords such as “apartments for sale in Colombo.” Technical SEO issues including duplicate titles and slow loading listing images were also fixed. A blog section covering property buying tips in Sri Lanka was introduced.

Result:
Organic website traffic increased 51% within four months. Average session time increased from 1 minute to 2 minutes 18 seconds, and direct property inquiries through the website increased from 19 to 33 leads per month.
