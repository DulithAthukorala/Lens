Summary: A Colombo accounting firm grew new client acquisitions by optimising its local search presence and launching a content strategy targeting small business tax and compliance queries.

Client: Perera & Associates, Chartered Accountants, Dehiwala
Industry: Professional Services / Accounting & Tax

Problem:
The firm had a strong reputation among existing clients but struggled to attract new business outside of referrals. Its website had not been updated since 2020, loaded in over 8 seconds on mobile, and ranked on page 5 or beyond for searches such as "tax consultant Colombo" and "accounting firm Sri Lanka SME." No content was published to address common compliance questions that prospective clients were actively searching.

Solution:
The website was technically overhauled, reducing mobile load time from 8.3 seconds to 2.4 seconds. A blog was launched publishing practical guides on topics including VAT registration, income tax filing for freelancers, and audit requirements for private limited companies in Sri Lanka. The Google Business Profile was fully optimised with service categories, photos, and a review collection process introduced post-engagement.

Result:
Organic traffic increased by 260% over five months. The firm's Google Business Profile moved from unranked to position 3 for "accounting firm Dehiwala." New client enquiries from organic search grew from 1–2 per month to 14. PageSpeed score improved from 31 to 76.
