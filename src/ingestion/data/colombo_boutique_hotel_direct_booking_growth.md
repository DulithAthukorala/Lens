Summary: A Colombo boutique hotel increased direct website bookings after improving mobile page speed and simplifying the online reservation process.

Client: OceanView Boutique Hotel Colombo  
Industry: Hospitality / Boutique Hotel

Problem:
Although the hotel had strong ratings on travel platforms such as Booking.com, direct bookings through its website remained low. The mobile booking page loaded slowly and required multiple steps to complete a reservation.

Solution:
The booking process was simplified to reduce the number of required steps and improve mobile usability. Large hero images were compressed and server caching was implemented to improve loading speed. SEO improvements targeted keywords such as “boutique hotel Colombo ocean view.”

Result:
Mobile PageSpeed improved from 35 to 78. Direct website bookings increased 27% over three months, helping reduce reliance on third-party booking platforms and improving overall profit margins.
