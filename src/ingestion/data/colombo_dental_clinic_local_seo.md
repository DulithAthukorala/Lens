Summary: A Colombo dental clinic increased online appointment bookings by improving local SEO rankings and optimizing slow-loading service pages.

Client: SmileCare Dental Colombo  
Industry: Dental Clinic

Problem:
SmileCare Dental depended heavily on patient referrals and had limited online search visibility. The website ranked on the second page of Google for keywords such as “dentist Colombo.” The blog section had not been updated in over a year, and several service pages loaded slowly on mobile.

Solution:
Service pages for treatments such as dental implants and teeth whitening were rewritten with optimized headings and structured content. Monthly educational blog posts were introduced to address common dental concerns. Image sizes were reduced and caching was enabled to improve mobile page performance.

Result:
Within three months, the clinic ranked on the first page for three local dental search queries. Organic traffic increased 39%, and monthly online appointment bookings increased from 24 to 37 patients.
