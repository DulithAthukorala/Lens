Summary: A Colombo retail electronics store improved foot traffic and local search visibility after claiming its Google Business Profile, implementing local SEO, and launching a promotional WhatsApp broadcast strategy.

Client: Voltech Electronics, Nugegoda
Industry: Retail / Consumer Electronics

Problem:
The store stocked laptops, mobile phones, accessories, and repairs but was losing walk-in customers to larger chain retailers and online platforms such as Daraz. It had no digital presence beyond an unclaimed Google Business Profile with outdated contact information and zero reviews. Searches for "laptop store Nugegoda" and "phone repair Colombo south" returned no results for the store.

Solution:
The Google Business Profile was claimed and fully optimised with product categories, updated pricing ranges, photos of the storefront and stock, and accurate operating hours. Local SEO citations were built across Sri Lankan business directories. A WhatsApp broadcast list was established from existing customer contacts for weekly promotional messages on new arrivals and repair offers. A basic product landing page was built targeting high-intent accessory and repair searches.

Result:
Google Business Profile views increased to 3,900 per month within three months. Direction requests and call clicks from Google Search grew by 214%. The WhatsApp broadcast list reached 480 subscribers and achieved an average open rate of 71%. Monthly repair bookings increased by 58%. The store's Google rating reached 4.5 stars from 63 verified reviews within 90 days.
