Summary: A Colombo construction company improved qualified project enquiries after building a project portfolio website and implementing local SEO targeting high-intent homeowner search terms.

Client: Buildcraft Lanka, Battaramulla
Industry: Construction / Residential & Commercial Building

Problem:
Despite completing over 80 residential and commercial projects, the company had no website presence and relied entirely on word-of-mouth referrals. Competitors were appearing in searches for "house construction Colombo" and "building contractor Sri Lanka" while Buildcraft had zero organic visibility. Estimating time was also wasted on poorly qualified enquiries with no defined scope or budget.

Solution:
A portfolio website was built featuring completed project galleries, cost-per-square-foot guidance, and a structured project enquiry form that collected budget range, project type, and timeline. Local SEO was implemented targeting construction and renovation keywords across the Western Province. A Google Business Profile was created and populated with project photos and verified client reviews.

Result:
The website reached 1,200 monthly organic visitors within four months. Monthly qualified project enquiries increased from 3 to 19. Google Business Profile impressions averaged 4,800 per month. The structured enquiry form reduced unqualified consultation requests by 55%, saving an estimated 12 estimating hours per month.
